Monthly Investment Conferences, Speedy Efficiency

Tainan City Government has specially instituted monthly investment conferences,

convened by the mayor, to promote and facilitate investment. Through these

meetings, and by providing investors with expert, customized assistance from

dedicated case managers, the city administration is pushing hard to adjust the

city’s industrial structure and create the driving forces for the city’s next wave of

economic growth.

Tainan is pulling out all the stops to help overseas Taiwanese businesses bring

investment back to Taiwan. It has set up a single window service for business

registration, with provision for online applications, mobile payment, and diverse

information systems enabling easy public inquiry, to construct a positive and efficient

investment environment, and create visible future prospects for young people.
